1 Overview

A major U.S. municipality issued an RFP for a multi-year network infrastructure modernization project. The deal was significant — hundreds of facilities, complex technical requirements, and multiple competing vendors.

Our sales team wanted something to differentiate the proposal beyond technical specs and pricing. They asked: Could we include a personalized video message from the CEO?

The request landed on my desk with 48 hours until the submission deadline.

2 Challenge

This wasn't a standard executive video. The challenges were specific:

  • •Personalization at scale: The video needed to feel genuinely tailored to this client — not a generic corporate message with a city name dropped in.
  • •Technical credibility: The audience was IT leadership evaluating infrastructure vendors. The message had to demonstrate understanding of their actual environment and needs.
  • •CEO voice authenticity: The script had to sound like our CEO — her cadence, her phrases, her way of building trust. Not "executive-speak."
  • •Timeline: 48 hours from request to recording-ready script, including stakeholder review cycles.

3 Approach

Rapid intake: I pulled the RFP details from the sales team — client environment, pain points, competitive positioning, specific technical requirements. I needed to understand what mattered to this buyer.

Structure design: I built a four-part framework for the message:

  1. Personal connection — Acknowledge the specific initiative and its importance to the community
  2. Relevant experience — Demonstrate understanding of their environment complexity (without being generic)
  3. Four key differentiators — Specific reasons to choose us, mapped to their stated priorities
  4. Mission alignment — Close on shared purpose, not sales language

Voice calibration: I wrote in the CEO's natural speech patterns — shorter sentences, direct address, phrases she actually uses. Then refined through one review cycle with her executive assistant.

4 Sample Excerpt

Opening — Anonymized

"We're really excited about the opportunity to partner with [MUNICIPALITY] on your network refresh and modernization. We understand this is more than just replacing equipment. You're building a network that supports your mission to serve the community for years to come — something we know a lot about."

Differentiator Section — Anonymized

"I could give you a hundred reasons why I think [COMPANY] is the right partner. But let's focus on four that apply directly to your needs..."

Close — Anonymized

"All of this adds up to one thing: We make your job easier by getting the job done. Our approach is collaborative, transparent, and focused on your success. And we know why that matters — your network is the backbone of public safety, city services, community engagement, and everything that keeps [MUNICIPALITY] running."

5 Impact

We won the contract. $19 million, multi-year engagement.

More importantly: during the down-select oral presentation, the client evaluation committee specifically cited the CEO video. According to the sales lead's email after the win:

"Your help — to create a personalized CEO video message for the client — was a game changer. It was the first thing the customer mentioned in our down-select oral presentation."

The format worked well enough that leadership requested a templated version for other public sector pursuits. The approach is now part of the standard playbook for high-value RFP responses.

What This Demonstrates

  • ✓ Speed without sacrificing quality: 48-hour turnaround on a script that moved a $19M deal
  • ✓ Voice matching: Writing that sounds like the CEO, not like "a CEO"
  • ✓ Strategic messaging: Structure designed to address specific buyer concerns, not generic benefits
  • ✓ Business impact: Executive communications that directly influenced a deal outcome
